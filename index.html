About Me

Hello! My name is Amy. I am a Sophomore at The University of Michigan currently pursuing a Computer Science major through the College of Literature, Science, and the Arts. Outside of computer science, my hobbies include cooking, painting, and snowboarding.

Last year, I completed an introductory computer science course offered at UofM. Within this course I was able to not only learn how to build several projects including a simulated battleship game, I was also given strong foundational skills on how to approach programming problems. The course was taught in C++ and I was able to work with a variety of group members throughout the semester. From this class and my others I have gained the ability to collaborate effectively despite the difficult virtual setting of the class. This year I am furthing my CS knowledge by taking Data Structures and Algorithms, Discrete Math, and Applied Computational Machine Learning among others.
